Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about finding cleaning jobs or hiring cleaners with Shinely.

Download the Shinely app, create a free cleaner account, and browse available cleaning jobs in your area. You can filter jobs by distance (5, 10, 25, or 50 miles), job type (residential, commercial, Airbnb), and price.

Cleaners on Shinely keep 90% of every job payment. Most cleaners earn between $20–$40 per hour depending on job type, location, and experience. Full-time cleaners on Shinely can earn $2,000–$4,000/month.

Downloading the app and creating an account is completely free. Cleaners pay a small 10% platform fee only when they complete a paid job — no monthly fees, no subscriptions required.

Standard payouts arrive in your bank account within 2–5 business days, completely free. Need money faster? Our instant payout option delivers funds in approximately 30 minutes for a small 1.5% fee.

Yes! Shinely has a Helper role designed for cleaners without transportation. You team up with a cleaner who has a car. The cleaner earns 70% of the job payment and the helper earns 30%. It's a great way to build experience and income without needing your own vehicle.

All cleaners on Shinely create verified accounts and are rated by homeowners after every job. You can see ratings, reviews, and job history before hiring anyone. Our community-driven rating system ensures consistent quality.
